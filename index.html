TechXplore

Rounds:

Opportunity
Odessey

Opportunity Odessey

Details:

Event Head : Vaishnavi Hemke
The event will be divided into two parts:
1. Beginner
2. Advanced
Rounds:
1) Aptitude Test
2) Code Debugging
Round 1: Aptitude test: This round will be MCQ based which will be based on simple aptitude questions. There will be total 15 questions out of which any 9 should be correct. Time limit for solving will be 15 minutes.

Round 2: Coding debug: This round will be about debugging a given set of codes and writing it again according to them what will be the right code in 20 minutes.

Round 3: Placement round: In this round the selected participants will be facing and answering the challenging questions asked by the experts present on the judging panel. These questions will be based on their knowledge about any subject, their way of communication, and honestly to the point answering.

Youth Parliament

Youth Parliament

Details:

Event Head : Dhanshree Gedam
Rounds:
1) Debate Competition(Day -1)
2) Mock Parliament(Day - 2)
Round 1:- Debate Competition
Participants will be divided into teams. (number of teams will vary according to the intake of participants.) Each team will consist of 10 members(may vary) and they would be given a topic to debate upon. Teams will be given a preparatory time of half an hour(1/2 hour). The best debaters in the respective teams will be qualified for round 2.

Round 2:- Mock Parliament
Participants qualified in the first round will be given a topic on the very first day to prepare. There would be only 2 main teams (1 opposition and 1 in favour). Participants will be divided into teams on time and on a fair basis. Participants from both the teams engage in arguments, questions, rebuttals and presentation of predetermined topics which must be backed by facts. The best 2 students from each team will be the winners.(total =4)

Bollywood
Carnival

Bollywood carnival

Details:

Event Head : Sujal Gayakwad
Rounds:
1) Emojis War
2) Whisper Challenge
3) Face Guesstimate
Round 1:- Emojis War
In this Round we will allow one plyer of a team to choose one option from the given set of options.Then the selected option will contain the emojis which will contain a movie name and then the player will explain the movie name to group members by acting. The time limit for this round will be 1 minute. The team which will guess more movies will be qualified for te second round.


Round 2:- Whisper Challenge
In this round, the qualified team will be performing the task in which one player of team will wear headphones and the songs will start playing loudly. Then the team members will explain the dialogue by lip-syncing. The time limit for this round will be 1 minute. The team which will guess more dialogues will be qualified for te second round.


Round 3:- Face Guesstimate
Here, bollywood movie characters will be shown in pairs and team members have to guess max number of movies. The time limit will be 45 seconds. The team that guesses the maximum number of movies will be the winner of "Bollywood Carnival".

Treasure Hunt

Treasure Hunt

Details:

Event Head:- Vishesh Thool
Round 1:
A treasure hunt involves teams or individuals searching for clues or hidden objects across various locations on class, department and campus. It has been structured with three rounds, each with its own games and levels.Throughout the game, teams or individuals earn points based on how quickly they solve the clues and complete the challenges. The team or individual with the highest score or fastest time at the end of the event is declared the winner of the treasure hunt.


Round 2:
The second round of game will involve a dark room where alphabets will be hidden. Each member of the group has to pick a chit, in which words will be written. Turn by turn the members has to go inside the dark room and find the letters of the word written in their chit. The team who collects more letters similar to their word given will be qualified for the final round.


Round 2:
In round three, each team will be given a chit written clues, the team has to decode the clue and find the next chit for next clue. This will be continued till the last clue chit that is fourth clue chit, in which the clue for the treasure will be given. The team who first finds the treasure (which will be common for all the teams) will be declared the winner of the game and will be awarded.
